Welcome!

Sweet Control is a web-based system designed to help with edible products inventory management.

You can register different Ingredients types and their respective Inventories informations, including total and left amounts, price and expiration date.

Beyond that, You can register your own Recipes , with informations about Ingredients and their amounts.
Sweet Control will keep track of how many times You can still cook your Recipes and how much the next cooking will cost ( Ingredients wise).

Also, You can follow up your Cooked Recipes , knowing which Inventories and amounts were used, making date and cost.
